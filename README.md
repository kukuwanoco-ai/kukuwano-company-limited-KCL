# Kukuwano Company Limited (KCL)

Welcome to the official repository of **Kukuwano Company Limited (KCL)**.  
We are an entrepreneurial company headquartered in Kajo‑Keji County, Central Equatoria State, South Sudan. Our mission is to build digital infrastructure, strengthen branding, and deliver investor‑ready solutions across East Africa.
